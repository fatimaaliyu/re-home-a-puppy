Re-home a puppy

Re-home a puppy is a service in London area helping puppies that need a home.This could be litters that need their first home or second home puppies that haven't been able to stay with their first owners and need a second chance.

Register

The first step towards re-homing one of puppies is the register with us. Once you have completed the form below one of our team will contact you for an initial conversation and arrange a home visit.
